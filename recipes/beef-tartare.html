Beef Tartare

Description

Step 1

To make the beef tartare, first clean the meat from any nerves and fatty parts, then cut it first into strips and then into half-cm cubes.
Transfer the meat to a bowl and season with salt, pepper, oil and Tabasco.
Add the Worcestershire sauce and mix. Now clean and chop the shallot. Also chop the desalted capers, gherkins and parsley.

Step 2

You are ready to plate: use a pastry cutter to give the meat the shape of a medallion, then complete the dish with the condiments, forming quenelles with the help of 2 teaspoons.
Then add the shallot and gherkins. Continue with the parsley, capers and mustard.
Finally, with the tip of the teaspoon, create a hollow on the surface of the tartare 19 and gently slide a yolk into the center
Your beef tartare is ready to be served

Ingredients:

  • Beef rump 220 g
  • Shallot 1
  • Capers in salt 20 g
  • Gherkins in vinegar 2
  • Dijon mustard 1 tablespoon
  • Egg yolks 2
  • Parsley 1 bunch
