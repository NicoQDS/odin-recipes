Spaghetti alla carbonara

Description

Step 1

Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil for the pasta.
Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat; add the guanciale.
Cook the guanciale until well browned and crispy, 5 to 10 minutes.
Remove from the heat and drain the guanciale on paper towels.
In a bowl, beat the egg yolks, half the Pecorino Romano, and a little black pepper until smooth and creamy; set aside.

Step 2

Cook the spaghetti in the boiling water, stirring occasionally, until tender but firm to the bite (al dente). Save some of the pasta cooking water!
Drain the pasta and return it to the pot. After a few seconds, pour the egg mixture over the pasta, stirring constantly, then add the remaining pecorino cheese and 1/2 cup of the cooking water.
Continue stirring until the egg mixture and starchy water have resulted in a creamy sauce that coats the spaghetti, stir in the bacon.

Step 3

Complete the Dish and Serve
Serve the spaghetti carbonara hot with a little extra black pepper to taste!

Ingredients:

  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil 2 tablespoons
  • Bacon 200 grams
  • Spaghetti 350 grams
  • Eggs (Yolk) 4
  • Pecorino 10 tablespoons
  • Salt to taste
  • Black Pepper to taste
